## What this single cron does
Laravel schedule:run checks the app every 5 minutes and runs:
  - invoices:send-service-reminders  → early alerts (5 min / 1 hr / 5 days) + repeat (weekly, monthly, etc.)
  - appointments:send-reminders      → daily at 9:00 AM

You do NOT need separate cron jobs per repeat interval. Weekly/monthly/yearly
is stored on each invoice and advanced automatically after the service date passes.
